Rain, Rain, Go away!

So, it's raining ... again. You forgot your umbrella ... again.

To top things off, you are, in fact, made of sugar. How did we get here?

Ingredients:

  • Low pressure meteorological conditions.
    • Clouds, lots of clouds.
    • Water, lots of water.
  • You don't own a car.
  • You live 30 minutes away by foot.
  • Uber prices are surging.
    • The same goes for Lyft, Via, etc.
  • Umbrellas are sold out everywhere.

Steps:

  1. Wake up at 8:37am. Shit, you're going to be late to work.
  2. Stumble out of bed, pull on whatever clothes are nearby. Inevitably you end up wearing mix-matched socks.
  3. Put bread in the toaster. Get distracted. Burnt toast for breakfast; scrumptious.
  4. Crucially, forget to check the weather. Leave umbrella at home.
  5. Arrive at work 10 minutes late, get scorned by boss.
  6. Settle into your temporary coffin (sometimes referred to as an 'office cubicle').
  7. After seven hours, free yourself from the shackles of labor.
  8. Exit office with a large smile on your face.
  9. Get dripped on. "Oh it's nothing, just a little drizzle."
  10. Get caught in torrential rains. REPENT!
  11. Slowly melt, returning to your original form: sugar.
  12. Embrace the warm arms of Thanatos.
