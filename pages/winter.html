Why are you invading Russia in the winter?

It's been tried. Many times, actually.

So many times there's an entire wikipedia entry dedicated to this very to topic.

Ingredients:

  • 10 ounces of hubris.
  • 20 ounces of ignorance.
  • A sprinkle of idiocy.
  • A boatload of frostbite.
  • Many, many frozen tears.

Steps:

  1. Forget about the countless times this has been tried before. To do so, combine the 10 ounces of hubris with the 20 ounces of ignorance.
  2. Using the sprinkle of idiocy, disregard the many advisors begging you to rethink your decision.
  3. Blaze onwards, with reckless abandon. At the end of the day, it's not you out on the battlefield.
  4. This is where the final two ingredients get introduced: frostbite and tears. Stir thoroughly.
  5. Once all mixed together, collapse onto your knees in the recognition that you have successfully completed a recipe for disaster.
    • Success, in this scenario, actually means failure: you've successfully failed to invade Russia during the winter. Well done.
  6. You can now rest easy at night, knowing your name will be added to the aformentioned wikipedia entry. Sleep well, sweet prince.
